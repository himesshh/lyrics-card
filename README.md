# Lyrics Card
##### Search any song, pick your favorite lines, and generate a clean shareable lyrics card; styled with YouTube Music branding.
---
#### Features
* Search songs or artists and get results instantly.
* Click a song to load its full lyrics.
* Select up to 4 lines to feature on your card.
* Customize background color, text color, and card format.
* Download as PNG in one click.
